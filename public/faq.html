Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is the SCIEnT ?

    SCIEnT is a completely student run and Alumni funded facility where a multitude of tools, consumables and 24/7 workspace is provided to students projects.

  • Who can have access to facilities at SCIEnT ?

    SCIEnT is open for all students of all years of NIT Trichy. But you need to have a valid project to work on.

    •
  • What is SCIEnT Student Team ?

    It Coordinate the happenings in SCIEnT and act as a bridge between alumni and students. It is the immediate point of contact for students.

    •
  • How can I get biometric access ?

    Fill the access requisition form in the website. You will be contacted by SCIEnT Student Team.

    •
  • I am not in any tech club. Can I use SCIEnT ?

    Yes. There is no need for a student to be in tech club to use SCIEnT

    •
  • How can I get my project funded ?

    Submit your abstract by filling the project registration form in our website. After scrutiny from both alumni and student sides, the panel will decide about granting funds.

    •
  • Can I get a tool outside of SCIEnT ?

    Yes. But, you have to give an undertaking letter to the SCIEnT admin incharge/ student team head, that you will be responsible for incase of any damage or loss.

    •
  • What kind of projects are supported by scient ?

    Any project that involves electronic and mechanical tools.

  • Does scient act as a learning platform for doing projects ?

    Yes. We can provide mentorship for your projects.
