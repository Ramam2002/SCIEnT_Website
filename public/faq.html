Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is SCIEnT ?

    SCIEnT is a completely student-run and alumni funded facility where a multitude of tools and consumables are provided in a workspace that is open 24/7 for students to work on projects.

  • Who can access the facilities at SCIEnT ?

    SCIEnT is open to all students of all years of NIT Trichy.

    •
  • What is the SCIEnT Student Team ?

    The team coordinates the happenings in SCIEnT and acts as a bridge between alumni and students. It is the immediate point of contact for the students using the facility.

    •
  • How can I get biometric access ?

    Fill the access requisition form in the website. You will be contacted by a representative from the Student Team on how to receive access.

  • Can I use SCIEnT even if I don't belong to a Tech club ?

    Yes, SCIEnT can be used by every student of the college.

    •
  • How can I get my project funded ?

    Submit your abstract by filling in the project registration form on our website. After scrutiny from both the alumni and students, a panel will decide about granting funds.

    •
  • Can I take a tool outside SCIEnT ?

    Yes, but you will have to give an undertaking letter to the SCIEnT Admin in-charge or the student team head, stating that you will be responsible for any damage or loss to the tool.

    •
  • Does SCIEnT provide guidance while doing a project ?

    Yes. We can provide mentorship for your projects by connecting you with alumni in a similar field.
